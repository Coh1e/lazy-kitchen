# Packaging guide

## What MUST be portioned

- Whole spice combinations (DRY pack) → spice sachet + vacuum
- Ground spice combinations → small zip bag inside vacuum
- Wet sauces → vacuum bag (liquid mode) + freeze
- Cooked bases → cool, vacuum, freeze
- Marinated meat → meat + marinade in vacuum + freeze

## What NOT to portion

| Type | Reason |
|---|---|
| High-frequency liquid condiments (light soy, fish sauce, rice vinegar) | Used quickly, bottle works |
| High-frequency pastes (miso, doubanjiang) | Variable amounts |
| Base ingredients (egg, tofu, onion) | Preserve the human ritual |
| Fresh herbs (cilantro, mint, lime) | Spoils fast, buy fresh |

## Portion with caution

| Type | Reason |
|---|---|
| Sauces with fresh garlic | Fridge unsafe long-term, must freeze |
| Scallion oil / garlic oil | Don't keep at room temp |
| Liquid soup packs | Pre-freeze, then vacuum (else sucked into sealer) |
| Loose powder direct vacuum | Powder gets sucked in; bag in sachet first |

## Referenced SOPs

- SOP-VACUUM-DRY-001 — dry vacuum
- SOP-VACUUM-LIQUID-001 — liquid vacuum
- SOP-LABELING-001 — labeling

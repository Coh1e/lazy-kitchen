# Base ingredients

Source of truth: [data/ingredients.yaml](../../../data/ingredients.yaml).

## Key principles

- Don't pre-pack proteins or vegetables (preserves the human ritual)
- Use Chinese e-commerce pre-prepped forms via `cn_market_pre_prepped[]`
- Categorize by `procurement_mode`

## Quick survey

### Proteins
Eggs, tofu, chicken thigh, pork belly, beef brisket, sliced pork/beef, peeled shrimp, fish fillet

### Vegetables
Onion, daikon, cabbage, leafy greens, mushrooms, carrot, potato, tomato

### Grains
Rice, noodles, rice noodles, udon, pasta

Full fields (zh+en names, aliases, storage, procurement, pre-prep, buying notes) in the yaml.

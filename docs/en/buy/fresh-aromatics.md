# Fresh aromatics

Source of truth: [data/fresh-aromatics.yaml](../../../data/fresh-aromatics.yaml).

## Three tiers

### Always keep
Ginger, garlic, scallion (high frequency)

### Buy when needed
Cilantro, lime, mint, basil, Thai basil

### Skip in MVP (specialty only)
Lemongrass, galangal, makrut lime leaves — rare in Chinese kitchens; substitute with commercial Thai curry pastes

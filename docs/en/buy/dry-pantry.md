# Dry pantry

Source of truth: [data/dry-pantry.yaml](../../../data/dry-pantry.yaml).

This layer is LK's strategic core — **bulk-buy dry spices, then portion into SKUs by formula**.

## Whole spices
Star anise, bay leaf, cassia bark, white peppercorn, Sichuan peppercorn, dried tangerine peel, dried chili

## Ground spices
Cumin, paprika, smoked paprika, chili powder, curry powder, turmeric, garlic/onion powder, black/white pepper

## Dried umami
Dashi powder, dried shiitake, wakame, kombu

## Why bulk

- Whole spices sealed dark: 1–3 year flavor window
- Ground spices: 6–12 months before fading
- Bulk + reasonable cycle = lower unit cost + better flavor

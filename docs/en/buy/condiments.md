# Base condiments

Source of truth: [data/condiments.yaml](../../../data/condiments.yaml).

Large-bottle stocked, NOT broken into SKUs.

## Quick survey (~20 items)

- **Salty-umami**: light soy, dark soy, oyster sauce, fish sauce, salt
- **Acid**: rice vinegar, black vinegar (Chinkiang), bottled lemon juice
- **Sweet**: white sugar, rock sugar, honey
- **Oils**: neutral cooking oil, toasted sesame oil, olive oil
- **Pastes**: Pixian doubanjiang, miso, gochujang, Thai red/green curry paste
- **Canned**: tomatoes, coconut milk

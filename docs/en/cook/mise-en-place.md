# Mise en place

> Escoffier's kitchen discipline: **all materials in place before fire on**.

## Why

- Chinese stir-fry especially: once the pan heats up, mid-cook chopping is too late
- Eliminates "oh wait I forgot..." panic moments
- Lets `hands_on_min` actually match what the yaml says

## How

### Before lighting fire

1. Read the entire SOP
2. List all needed ingredients + condiments + SKU packs
3. Cut, measure, plate everything in small bowls
4. Prepare the serving vessel (plate or bowl)
5. Cloth, trivet, spatula within arm's reach

### Plating layout

```
Bowl 1: garlic + ginger + scallion (one-pot pour)
Bowl 2: meat + marinade (separate)
Bowl 3: vegetables (in cooking order)
Bowl 4: seasonings (soy + vinegar + sugar pre-measured)
Bowl 5: finishers (slurry + sesame oil, last second)
```

### Then light the fire

Follow SOP. No WeChat, no phone calls.

## SKU benefit

If you're using LK's DRY/WET packs:
- DRY pack = your "spice bowl" condensed
- WET pack = your "marinade bowl" condensed
- Cutting (CUT-*) mostly delegated to e-commerce pre-prep

→ Mise en place compresses to 5 minutes.

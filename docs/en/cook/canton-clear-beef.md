## Steps

1. Place beef brisket in cold water; bring to boil, blanch 3–5 minutes
2. Remove, rinse off scum with warm water, drain, cut into ~5cm cubes
3. To soup pot: 1.5–2L water + beef + 3 ginger slices + spice pack
4. Bring to a hard boil, then drop to a bare simmer with lid ajar; simmer 90–150 minutes
5. Add daikon (large cubes) for the last 25–40 minutes
6. Season at end: salt + a touch of fish sauce + ~5g rock sugar; taste
7. Top with chopped scallion; serve over rice noodles, flat noodles, or rice

## Key tips

- Blanch from cold water; hot start traps scum inside the meat
- Bare simmer with lid ajar keeps broth clear; rolling boil = cloudy broth
- Daikon goes in late or it turns bitter
- Use fish sauce sparingly — it deepens umami without imposing its own flavor

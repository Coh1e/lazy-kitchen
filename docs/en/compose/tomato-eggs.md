# Tomato and eggs / 番茄炒蛋

<span class="status status-proposed">proposed</span> <span class="cuisine">中 · CN</span>

> ✦ The quintessential beginner Chinese home-cook dish; 5 min hands-on, no SKU/Ratio needed.

## Composition formula

```
Fresh ingredients + SOP-STIR-FRY-001 = a meal
```

## Ingredients (2 servings)

| Item | Amount | Note |
|---|---|---|
| Tomato | 2 medium (~300 g) | Stemmed, cut into 6–8 wedges each |
| Egg | 3 large (~150 g) | Beaten; optionally add 1 tsp Shaoxing wine |
| Chopped scallion | 1 handful (~10 g) | Separate white (into pan first) and green (sprinkled at end) |
| Cooking oil | 2 Tbsp (~30 ml) | Used in two additions, 1 Tbsp (~15 ml) each |
| Salt | 1 tsp (~5 g) | Added after tomato releases juice |
| Sugar | 1 tsp (~4 g) | Balances tomato acidity |

## Time

| Phase | Minutes |
|---|---|
| Prep | 3 |
| **Hands-on** | **5** |
| Unattended | 0 |
| Total | 8 |

## Flavor structure (Salt Fat Acid Heat)

- **Salt**: salt + 1 tsp sugar to round the acid
- **Fat**: 2 Tbsp cooking oil + egg yolk fat
- **Acid**: tomato's natural acidity
- **Heat**: high stir-fry, 5 min total

## Hardware

✅ MVP 10-piece kit suffices (wok + chef knife + spatula)

## Steps (from SOP-STIR-FRY-001)

1. **Prep**: stem and wedge tomatoes; beat eggs with a pinch of salt; separate scallion white and green.
2. **Cook eggs**: heat 1 Tbsp (~15 ml) oil over high; pour in eggs. Scramble loosely; remove when just set but still soft (~30 s). Reserve.
3. **Cook tomatoes**: add remaining 1 Tbsp (~15 ml) oil, scallion white for 5 s, then tomato wedges. Stir-fry over medium-high until edges soften and red juices release (~2 min).
4. **Season**: add 1 tsp (~5 g) salt and 1 tsp (~4 g) sugar; toss to dissolve.
5. **Combine**: return eggs; toss over high heat for 30 s so eggs coat with tomato juice.
6. **Finish**: sprinkle scallion green; off heat, plate.

## Key points

- Eggs should be soft-set when removed (≈70–80% cooked); over-cooked eggs become rubbery.
- Tomatoes MUST release their juice — that's the sauce.
- Sugar isn't for sweetness; it rounds the tomato's acid.
- For a richer texture: score tomatoes with a knife cross, blanch in hot water 30 s to remove skin first (adds 1 min).

## Reuse

- SOP-STIR-FRY-001 is also used by: kung pao chicken, mapo tofu return-fry, green pepper pork.

# Old duck soup

<span class="status status-proposed">**Status**: Proposed</span>

## Ingredients
- 1 whole old duck (about 1500 g)
- 30 g ginger
- 15 g goji berries
- 30 ml Huadiao wine
- 5 g salt
- 2 g white pepper powder

## Steps
1. **Prepare duck**: Clean and cut duck into large pieces. Blanch in cold water to remove impurities, drain.
2. **Sauté ginger**: Heat a little oil, sauté ginger slices, add duck pieces, stir-fry until lightly browned.
3. **Simmer**: Add enough boiling water (about 2 L). Bring to a boil, skim off foam, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 1.5 hours.
4. **Season**: Add goji berries, Huadiao wine, salt, and white pepper. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
5. **Serve**: Soup turns golden, duck is tender. Serve hot.

⚠ If using young duck, reduce simmering time to 45 minutes.
